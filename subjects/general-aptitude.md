## âš ï¸ Common Mistakes

1. **RC: Don't read extra meaning:** Stick strictly to what the passage says â€” no outside knowledge
2. **Work problems:** Don't directly add days; add RATES (work/day)
3. **Profit on cost or selling price?** Usually on cost price unless specified otherwise
4. **Relative speed:** Same direction = difference; opposite direction = sum
5. **Pie chart:** Convert percentage to degrees by multiplying by 3.6
6. **Compound interest formula:** (1 + r/n)^(nt) for n compounding periods/year
7. **Assumptions in CR:** An assumption must be unstated AND necessary for the conclusion
8. **Syllogism:** Use Venn diagrams; "some" means at least one; "no" means zero overlap
